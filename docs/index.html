Main Objectives

  • Saving Money

    The aspect of saving one’s money is applicable to people from all walks of life;from students to office going professionals.Considering money to be a resource,every resource tend to get depleted over a period of time.Here we try to work towards such a problem statement.

  • Visualise The Inflow and Outflow of Money

    Saving the expense and income just solves one part of the problem statement.To get an in depth understanding of the monetary flow graphs with various filters such as date,categories,mode of payment is necessary.

  • Working in Harmony

    To work as a team and sharing the tasks among ourselves is one of the main objectives of our team members.Active Coordination of team members leads to a smooth completion of the project

  • Meeting User Requirements

    Developing the application corresponding to the user requirements is very necessary to obtain their satisfaction and further inputs.So this stands as a the main motto over which our project is built on.
